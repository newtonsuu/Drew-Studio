Drew Studio offers a diverse range of products and services that cater to all your photography needs. From studio photography packages to specialized services, we are here to turn your memories into timeless works of art. Explore our offerings today and let us help you create magic through the lens. Discover how our expertise can transform you and leave a lasting impression.

Package 1

This photography package offers a 2-hour photoshoot with unlimited edited digital photos for individuals, couples, or families up to 5 people. The photographer will take an average of 30 shots per hour, and you can extend the session for an additional 5,000 yen (around 2,000 Philippine pesos) per hour.

Package 2

Priced at ¥25,000 (around P10,000). This package includes a 2-hour photoshoot with unlimited photos (average of 30 per hour) for groups or families of 6-10 people. All the edited soft copies of the photos are included in the package. There is an additional charge of ¥6,000 (around P2,400) per hour if you want to extend the photoshoot and receive all the edited soft copies.

Package 3

This photography package caters to larger groups and families of over 10 people. For ¥30,000 (around P12,000), you'll receive a 2-hour photoshoot with unlimited photos (averaging 30 per hour), all delivered as edited digital copies. If you'd like to extend the session, it's an additional ¥7,000 (around P2,800) per hour.

Package 4

This photography package costs ¥30,000 (around P12,000). It includes a 24-hour video shoot, a save-the-date style video, and a 5k (12k) per rr extension. The details of the rr extension are not mentioned in the image.

Package 5

The photography package is to be priced at ¥20,000 (around P8,000). It includes a 3-4 hour photoshoot with both photo and video coverage. You will also receive a 3-5 minute save-the-date style video. Transportation to Tokyo is included, with additional charges for transportation to other locations. There is also an extra fee of ¥4,000 per hour if the photoshoot goes past 6pm.
